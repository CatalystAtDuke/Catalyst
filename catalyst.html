Interested in Joining Catalyst?

Upcoming Rush Events

Rush is a 3 week long process starting in late September and October.
The process consists of two rounds separated by cuts. Those who are invited to the next round will be notified by email.
Rush is comprised of a mixture of social and educational events.
Rushees will need to submit both a short application and attend an interview.

Member of the Week

Cody Li

Class of 2018

Major: Computer Science

Our member of the week this week is Cody Li, a junior pursuing a major in Computer Science and a minor in Electrical and Computer Engineering. A North Carolina native, his interests are in computer architecture, computer hardware, and big data. He is spending the summer of 2016 working at the Duke University School of Medicine. There, he helps develop software to aid with smoking cessation research. His most recent project is an iOS app that records facial expressions in order to generate data about patient reactions.
