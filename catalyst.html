Upcoming Rush Events

Rush is a 3 week long process starting in late September and October.
The process consists of two rounds separated by cuts. Those who are invited to the next round will be notified by email.
Rush is comprised of a mixture of social and educational events.
Rushees will need to submit both a short application and attend an interview.
